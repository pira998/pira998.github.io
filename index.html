Piraveen Sivakumar

I am a Machine Learning Engineer at H2O.AI with a strong interest in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, specifically focusing on 3D reconstruction models. My academic background includes a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Moratuwa.

During my undergraduate studies, I conducted my Bachelor's Thesis under the guidance of Dr. Thanuja Ambegoda, where I worked on the reconstruction of 3D objects using only single or few views with the assistance of NeRF and meta-learning techniques.

At H2O.AI, I've had the privilege of working on exciting projects, such as Driverless AI and the creation of GenAI AppStudio with features like Sketch2app and prompt2app. My professional journey also encompasses a wide array of Data Science projects.

GenAI AppStudio

GenAI AppStudio is a platform that allows users to create AI applications without writing a single line of code using sketches or prompts. It offers seamless integration with h2oGPT for natural language interaction within your apps. These apps feature multiple pages for a user-friendly experience and utilize real, accurate data to provide users with relevant information. Additionally, the built-in studio allows for easy customization, and you can download the complete source code for your convenience.
